* 1) If the user's input is one word, it will capitalize that word.
    ex: beowulf -> Beowulf

* 2) If the user's input is multiple words, it will captitalize all words.
    ex: star trek -> Star Trek

3) In multiple word user input, small words will be lower case.
    ex: tale of two cities -> Tale of Two Cities

4) If small word is at the beginning of user input, it should be capitalized.
    ex: the lion, the witch, and the wardrobe -> The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe

5) It words are both capitalized and lower case within each word, return the title with only the first letter capitalized
    ex: sTaR WaRS -> Star Wars
